EST. ROOTS IN NORTHERN VIRGINIA RUGBY

Virginia's Premier
D2 & D3 Club

Riverlands Rugby Football Club, known as the Golden Boars, is a competitive men's rugby club competing in Premier Division 2 and Division 3 in Northern Virginia.

We are a passionate, hard-working group of players dedicated to the sport of rugby, competing at a high level while fostering a strong sense of brotherhood and community both on and off the pitch.
