OUR LEGACY

History of the
Golden Boars

From Rappahannock roots to championship contenders in Northern Virginia rugby.

Riverlands Rugby Football Club, competing as the Golden Boars, has deep roots in the Northern Virginia rugby community. Originally founded as Rappahannock Rugby, the club has evolved into a competitive force in both Division 2 and Division 3 of the Capitol Rugby Union (CRU).

THE NAME & IDENTITY

Golden Boars

The "Golden Boars" name reflects the fierce, tenacious spirit of the team. The boar symbolizes strength, courage, and determination — qualities embodied by every player who wears the green and gold.

Affiliation: Capitol Rugby Union (CRU)
Divisions: Premier D2 & D3
NOTABLE

Strong connection to the broader Rappahannock rugby community
